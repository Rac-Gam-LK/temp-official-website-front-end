Welcome to the Rotaract Club of Gampaha, a dynamic community of young professionals and students dedicated to making a positive impact locally and globally. Chartered in January 2024 under the leadership of Charter Club President Rtr. Lehindu Atapattu, we proudly stand as the first Rotaract club in Gampaha town and the second self-sponsored Rotaract club in Sri Lanka and the Maldives. Our foundation marks a significant milestone in the Rotaract District 3220, demonstrating our commitment to Innovation, Growth, Retention, and Sustainability.

Our Vision and Mission

At the Rotaract Club of Gampaha, we believe in the power of innovation and collaboration to drive meaningful change. Our vision focuses on fostering growth, ensuring member retention, and promoting sustainable practices within our community and beyond. We are passionate about volunteering and seek to inspire others to join our mission of service and development.

Our Journey

Reflecting on our inaugural year, we are proud of the projects we've undertaken and the reputation we've begun to build. Our diverse membership brings together individuals from various backgrounds and disciplines, united by a shared passion for service.

Our Projects and Initiatives

We have been actively involved in numerous service projects, ranging from community development initiatives to international partnerships. Our projects are designed to address pressing social issues, promote sustainable development, and empower individuals to reach their full potential.

Join Us

If you are over 18 and passionate about volunteering, we invite you to join the Rotaract Club of Gampaha. Together, we can create a better world, build lasting connections, and inspire others to take action. Through our collective efforts, we strive to make a significant impact and pave the way for a brighter future.
