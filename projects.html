Avurudu '24

A Festive Fusion of Sinhala and Tamil New Year Traditions
| 23rd April 2024 | 9.30AM - 4.30PM | Liyara Nature Farm Resorts |

The project "අවුරුදු 24'" aimed to celebrate the traditional Sinhala and Tamil New Year, through a series of culturally rich and engaging activities. The project sought to bring together club members with the spirit of festivity and unity.
The event featured games such as pebbles on a yoghurt spoon, balloon dance, blindfold yoghurt, and traditional favorites like අලියට ඇස (Aliyata Esa) and තුන්පා දිවීම (Thunpa Diwima). Additionally, participants enjoyed catching water-filled balloons, පැණි බඹරය, බැලූන් පිපිරවීම, and බෝතලයට වතුර පිරවීම. The fun continued with a paper dance, සැඟවුණු අමුත්තා, musical hat, and card games. The project team meticulously crafted a detailed schedule to ensure a smooth flow of activities, adding a festive spirit of unity among club members.

Career Compass

Navigate Your Career Journey For Success
| 27th Jan, 24th Feb, 3rd March 2024 | 8PM - 9PM | Online |

The Career Compass initiative is a comprehensive series of sessions designed to provide end-to-end guidance for students, undergraduates, youth, and job seekers. The thoughtfully curated topics ensure a holistic approach to career development, covering essential areas such as self-reflection, career exploration, and goal setting. The program includes detailed strategies for choosing a major, exploring educational pathways, and effectively navigating job searches across various industries like IT, Banking & Finance, Design & Architecture, and FMCG.
Participants will benefit from sessions on personal branding, professional etiquette, and continuous learning, which are crucial for career advancement and lifelong learning. The series also addresses the importance of adapting to changing career landscapes by developing transferable skills, embracing entrepreneurship, and freelancing opportunities. Additionally, the program focuses on navigating challenges and building resilience, offering guidance on overcoming setbacks, achieving work-life balance, and coping with change. This initiative aims to equip individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their professional and personal journeys.
