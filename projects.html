Bridging Horizons

Paving the Way from Interact to Rotaract
| Nov 1st | 8pm - 9pm | Online |

In an inspiring collaboration, the Rotaract Club of Gampaha teamed up with the Interact Club of Bandaranayake College, Gampaha, to launch the 'Bridging Horizons' project. This virtual gathering marked a significant step towards guiding young Interact members through the transition to Rotaract, offering them a window into the vast opportunities that Rotaract presents. The session was not only a platform for learning but also a beacon of inspiration for the next generation of leaders. The session covered three areas and it was done by Rtr. IPP Sanara Gamage.

Heal the Bay

A Testament to Community and Environmental Stewardship
| Oct 14th | 6.30am - 8.30am| CrowIsland Beach |

In an impactful display of environmental commitment and community spirit, the Rotaract Clubs of Colombo Central and Gampaha spearheaded the 'Heal the Bay' initiative, a beach clean-up event at Crow Island Beach, Colombo. This collaborative effort underscored the vital importance of preserving marine ecosystems and highlighted the detrimental effects of pollution on our natural habitats.

Avurudu '24

A Festive Fusion of Sinhala and Tamil New Year Traditions
| April 23rd | 9.30AM - 4.30PM | Liyara Nature Farm Resorts |

The project "අවුරුදු 24'" aimed to celebrate the traditional Sinhala and Tamil New Year, through a series of culturally rich and engaging activities. The project sought to bring together club members with the spirit of festivity and unity.
The event featured games such as pebbles on a yoghurt spoon, balloon dance, blindfold yoghurt, and traditional favorites like අලියට ඇස (Aliyata Esa) and තුන්පා දිවීම (Thunpa Diwima). Additionally, participants enjoyed catching water-filled balloons, පැණි බඹරය, බැලූන් පිපිරවීම, and බෝතලයට වතුර පිරවීම. The fun continued with a paper dance, සැඟවුණු අමුත්තා, musical hat, and card games. The project team meticulously crafted a detailed schedule to ensure a smooth flow of activities, adding a festive spirit of unity among club members.
