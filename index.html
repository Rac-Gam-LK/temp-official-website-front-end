Welcome to Rotaract Gampaha

We are a group of youngsters focused on Innovation, Growth, Retention & Sustainability. We are the first Rotaract club from Gampaha town and the second self-sponsored Rotaract club in Sri Lanka & Maldives. We are chartered in Jan 2024 by Charter Club President Rtr. Lehindu Atapattu. Avenues

Community Service

Caring for Communities Everywhere - Our community service avenue team focus on areas like Environmental Service, Health and Wellness, Education and Literacy, Community Development and Infrastructure, Hunger and Homelessness, Youth and Children Services, Elderly and Vulnerable Groups Support, Disaster Response and Relief, Cultural and Heritage Promotion (Local), Animal Welfare etc.

Professional Development

Skills for Success, Growth for Life - Our Professional Development Avenue team focuses on the following areas. Career Development, Skills Training and Workshops, Networking and Mentorship, Entrepreneurship and Innovation, Professional Ethics and Conduct, Financial Literacy and Management, Global Opportunities and Exchange, Industry-Specific Development, Personal Development, Technology and Digital Skills, Leadership Development etc.

Club
Service

Unity Through Service, Strength Through Fellowship - Areas focused by our club service team includes; Membership Development and Retention, Fellowship and Social Activities, Inter-Club Relations and Networking, Club Culture and Traditions, Diversity and Inclusion.

International Services

Connecting Continents through Cultural Beauty - Our International service avenue team focus on areas such as International Projects and Collaborations, Cultural Exchange Programs, Global Awareness and Education, Global Health Initiatives, International Fundraising and Support, International Friendship and Networking, Language and Communication Skills.

Other
Avenues

Service in Every Form, Impact in Every Act - Other than the four core avenues, our club focuses on avenues including PR, Regional Engagement, Fundraising and partnerships, Digital Coms (IT & Media)and Sports.

About Us

Welcome to Rotaract Club of Gampaha, a community of young professionals and students dedicated to making a positive impact in local and global communities. We have been actively engaged in various service projects, ranging from community development initiatives to international partnerships. Our diverse membership brings together individuals from different backgrounds and disciplines, united by a shared passion for service and a desire to create meaningful change. At Rotaract Gampaha, we believe in the power of collaboration and collective action. Through our projects and activities, we strive to address pressing social issues, promote sustainable development, and empower individuals to reach their full potential. Join us in our mission to make a difference, build connections, and inspire others to take action. Together, we can create a better world for all.
